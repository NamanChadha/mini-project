About Binge Buddy

Welcome to Binge Buddy - your ultimate Movie/TV Show Tracker!

Binge Buddy is a user-friendly app designed to help you keep track of your watched movies and TV shows, rate them, and create personalized watchlists.

With Binge Buddy, you can:

  • Keep track of all the movies and TV shows you've watched, including details like title, genre, and release year.
  • Rate them based on your personal preferences and add your own reviews to help others discover great content.
  • Create watchlists for future viewing, organize them by categories such as "Must-Watch," "Comedy," or "Action," and mark your progress as you watch.
  • Receive personalized recommendations based on your viewing history and ratings, helping you discover new movies and TV shows you're likely to enjoy.
  • Sync your data across multiple devices for easy access, ensuring that your watchlists and ratings are always up to date.
  • Explore trending movies and TV shows, popular genres, and top-rated content within the Binge Buddy community.

Whether you're a casual viewer or a dedicated binge-watcher, Binge Buddy is the perfect companion to organize your entertainment experience and make the most out of your viewing time.
